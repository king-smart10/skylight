About Us

Digitizing wealth management for Africans. We are building savings and investment culture among the growing population of underserved African middle class and millennials. Mission To enable members reach their financial or material objectives through simplified, affordable, accessible banking and consumer services. Vision To become the dream cooperative of every investor, meeting both individual and corporate needs.

THE JOURNEY

Port Harcourt Skylight Thrift Co-operative Investment and Credit Society Limited (PSYCHICAL, RS:32936) is people-oriented Co-operative Society based in Nigeria and provides microfinance products and services to members through thrift savings, soft loans, essential household needs (dba Skylight Co-operative). At Skylight, we combine innovation and integrity with organisational prowess and cooperative best practices, through which we render excellent services that empower our members. Inaugurated in 2011 as an in-house attempt to improve domestic welfare, Skylight has evolved through commitment and innovation under capable leadership, to become a model establishment in sustainable micro-savings. Our Services

  • Fixed Deposits

    This plan is similar to the daily thrift savings plan but differs in duration and pattern. The plan encourages members to save for longer periods (usually one year). It is patterned in such a way that withdrawal is discouraged until the duration completes. The plan also rewards members who diligently complete it with a 5% interest on the amount saved, paid at the end of the savings cycle. The amount saved in this plan is predefined by Skylight Cooperative. The offers currently available in this plan are shown in Appendix

  • flexible Deposits (Akawo)

    This plan avails users the opportunity to set aside a stipulated amount daily over some time, accrued monthly. The plan primarily rewards its participants with developing the discipline of daily savings and the realisation of how funds can be saved over time by setting a little aside consistently and on daily basis. The plan is designed such that each member decides how much to set aside daily. Skylight Cooperative provides an easy and innovative way of collecting and storing up the funds for the members over a 31-day period. The funds saved are available to the respective members at any time upon notice of withdrawal.

  • food contribution

    This plan is designed to help members stock up their food reserves by deploying a gradual installment system. The plan encourages daily or weekly contributions geared towards the acquisition of foodstuff. When the plan is completed, the member receives the food items listed in the respective plans. The amount saved in this plan and the duration of the plan depends on the quantity of foodstuff listed.

  • products/appliances savings

    This plan is similar to the Food item contribution plan. The plans defer, however, in the sense that rather than saving for food items, members save for household appliances or other products that they desire to own. These appliances and products may be cumbersome to buy for many families. This plan, therefore, enables the acquisition of desired appliances through the discipline and flexibility of daily savings. The products and appliances vary in size, cost and type, thus the daily amount and duration required to acquire them also varies.
